History of C

C programming language was developed in 1972 by Dennis Ritchie at bell laboratories of AT&T (American Telephone & Telegraph).C uses many concepts from ALGOL, BCPL and B languages and added the concept of datatypes and other powerful features.

For many years C was used mainly in academic environments, but with the release of many C compilers for commerical use and the increasing popularity of UNIX, it began to gain widespread support among computer professionals.Today C is running under a variety of operating system and hardware platforms.

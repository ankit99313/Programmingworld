Operator

It is a special symbol which is used to perform logical or mathematical operation on data or variable.

Types of operator:

Arithmetic operator

+, - , * , / , %

Relational operators

== Equal to
!= Not equal to
> Greater than
< Less than
>= Greater than equal to
<= Less than or equal

Logical operators

&& Logical AND
|| Logical OR
! Logical NOT

Assignment operators

=, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=

Bitwise operators

& Bitwise AND
| Bitwise OR
<< Shift Left
>> Shift Right

Increment/Decrement operators

++ Increment
--Decrement

Special operators

& -It is used find address of a variable.
* - It is used to declare pointer type variable.
sizeof() -It returns the memory size of variable.
